Milford Sound is the only fiord in New Zealand accessible by car. It is well known as one of the wettest inhabited places in the world with an average rainfall over 6000mm per year. So, don't forget your raincoat! With such heavy rainfall you can be sure to see the true beauty of this place. Rain creates dozens of beautiful waterfalls all over the fiord. The best way how to enjoy Milford Sound is a cruise. You can choose from many companies, but I can assure you that all tours are more or less the same. If you are lucky enough you might be able to see native wildlife such as fur seals or even Fiordland penguins. ğŸ§
